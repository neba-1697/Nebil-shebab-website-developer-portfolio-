My Journey

From Ethiopia to the world of web development

Self-Taught Developer

My journey began in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, where I discovered my passion for web development. With limited resources but unlimited determination, I taught myself programming through online courses, documentation, and countless hours of practice.

Learning web development in Ethiopia came with its challenges - unreliable internet, limited access to modern tools, and a scarcity of local mentors. But these obstacles only fueled my determination to succeed.

Growth Mindset

I believe in continuous learning and improvement. Every day, I dedicate time to expand my knowledge, experiment with new technologies, and refine my craft. My journey has taught me that with persistence and passion, anything is possible.
